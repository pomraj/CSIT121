Nepali Mo:Mo

MOMO Ghar Resturant

This Mo:mo specially made with chicken, Seasoning, pelled ginger, chopped onions,and black pepper.This Mo:mo for every people for a dish. Specially,it is eaten in India and Nepal. There are many ways to make this Nepali Mo:Mo, but my favorite recipe is little different and uses only a few Ingredients

Ingredients

  • Chopped Onion
  • Chicken things
  • Garlic
  • peeled ginger
  • black pepper
  • Chili Power
  • salt and cumin

Process and Direction to prepare

  1. Mix together flour and water in bowl.
  2. Meanwhile, Mix together chicken, cilantro, onions,garlic,ginger,cumin,cinnamon, salt & paper.
  3. To make wrappers: place the ball on a flat surface and roll it with a rolling pin.
  4. Spray a steamer pan with cooking spray.
  5. Fill the chicken in middle of a wrapper .
  6. Fill the steamer pot halfway with water and bring to a boil. Steam the Momos untill cooked.
